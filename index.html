How it works

Built to be simple

01
Choose your starting point
Pick your sport, your current grade (9th, 10th, or 11th), and the division level you're targeting. The plan adjusts to your situation.
02
Follow the roadmap
Work through phase-by-phase checklists covering academics, athletic development, coach outreach, visits, and signing — with NCAA compliance built in.
03
Your progress saves automatically
Every checkbox is saved to your browser. Come back anytime and pick up exactly where you left off — no account, no login, no setup.

Built for everyone in the process

Student athlete
You're in charge of your recruiting
The student planner puts you in the driver's seat — with a clear checklist, division-specific tasks, a full glossary, and progress tracking that keeps you accountable across all four years of high school.
Parent or guardian
Support without overstepping
The parent guide explains the process, your role year by year, the financial realities of scholarships, and exactly where well-meaning parents accidentally make things harder for their student.
Coach or counselor
Share it with your athletes
All tools are free, open source, and work offline. Share the URL, drop the HTML file into your LMS, or link it from your school's athletics page. No accounts, no friction for students.
